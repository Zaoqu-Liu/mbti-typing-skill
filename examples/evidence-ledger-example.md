## Contradiction Gate

- Best evidence for leading type: default ownership and corrective execution response.
- Best evidence against leading type: identity-level sensitivity to depth and meaning.
- Best evidence for runner-up: private model quality may matter more than external efficiency.
- Evidence runner-up cannot explain well: repeated external structuring without audience pressure.
- Role/culture/stress alternatives: achievement culture and work environment may amplify Te-like behavior.
- User corrections incorporated: do not type from ambition alone.
- Non-standard terms labeled: A/T is not official MBTI; Big Five is only a cross-check.
